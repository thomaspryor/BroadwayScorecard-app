## Description (4000 chars max)

Broadway Scorecard is the definitive guide to Broadway and West End theatre, powered by critic reviews from 400+ outlets.

WHAT YOU GET:
- Composite critic scores for every Broadway, Off-Broadway, and West End show
- Reviews from The New York Times, Variety, Vulture, The Guardian, and 400+ outlets
- Audience grades from Show-Score, Mezzanine, and Reddit
- Score breakdowns (positive/mixed/negative review counts)
- Cast lists, creative teams, and show details
- Ticket links to TodayTix, Telecharge, and more

FEATURES:
- Browse all currently playing shows, sorted by score or opening date
- Filter by musicals, plays, open shows, previews, or closed
- Switch between NYC (Broadway + Off-Broadway) and London (West End)
- Toggle between critic scores and audience grades
- Search by show title, theater, or creative team member
- Featured rows: Top Shows, Best Musicals, Best Plays, Closing Soon, Great for Kids
- Offline support — cached data works without internet

HOW SCORING WORKS:
Broadway Scorecard aggregates reviews from 400+ outlets and assigns each a score from 0-100. Reviews are weighted by outlet tier: top-tier outlets (NYT, Variety, Vulture) carry more weight than smaller publications. The composite score gives you an instant, reliable measure of critical reception.

Free. No account required. No ads.
